We are Creative Generation

A crew of young creators who believe that kids have something powerful to say — through stories, art, books, and ideas that can move the world.

Born from imagination,
built for the future

Creative Generation started with a simple belief: kids are not the future — they're the now. Every story we write, every painting we make, every idea we share is proof that young voices matter and deserve to be heard.

"We are the generation that creates — not just consumes. We write worlds into existence, paint what others cannot see, and ask the questions no one else dares to ask."
— The Creative Generation crew

What we believe
Every voice counts
Age is just a number. A 10-year-old's perspective is as valid and valuable as anyone else's. We publish without filters.
Creativity has no rules
Mix genres, break formats, combine art and writing — we celebrate the unexpected. The weirder, the better.
Together we go further
We cheer each other on, collaborate, and lift up every creator. Because the best work happens when we build each other up.
